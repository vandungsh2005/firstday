ABOUT VALORANT

IS VALORANT FREE TO PLAY?

Yes, Valorant is free to play and will probably stay that way. Valorant follows a similar Payment model as League of Legends does where you can buy cosmetic items in there in game shop which don’t give you any advantage in game.

WHAT KINDA GAME IS VALORANT?

Valorant is a competitive FPS similar to CS:GO but with Hero’s and abilities more comparable to Overwatch but more on that later.

WHAT CAN I DO TO PRACTICE MY AIM FOR VALORANT

The current optimal way of practicing your aim is by playing Valorant but besides that warming up in some Aim trainer such as KovaaK or Aim Lab's helps a lot too and is advised by our Coaches. For more Information on that stay up dated with our blog. We are planning on publishing a full warm up rotation performed by our best plays on a daily bases.

DOES VALORANT HAVE A RANKING SYSTEM

Yes, Valorant has a Valorant pretty similar to the one League of Legends has where you start of by playing 5 placement matches after which you get ranked based on your performance. Each ranks as 3 Tiers for example Gold 1, Gold 2 and Gold 3 all leading up to the Highs rank called Radiant.
